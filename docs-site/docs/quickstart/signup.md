# Sign up

You can sign up at <https://planscape.app/signup> — no credit card needed for the 30-day trial.

The signup form asks for:

| Field            | Notes                                                              |
|------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Organisation name| Used on invoices and transmittals                                  |
| Tenant slug      | `{slug}.planscape.app` — your team's URL                           |
| Plan             | Studio / Practice / Network — pick the closest fit; you can change |
| Country          | Auto-detects your billing currency                                 |
| Currency         | Override the auto-detect if needed (UGX/KES/TZS/RWF/NGN/ZAR/USD/EUR/GBP) |

Once you click **Create account** you're logged in immediately and the [onboarding wizard](first-project.md) walks you through the next steps.

## Trial state

A new tenant always starts on **Trial** for 30 days. We email you reminders 7, 3, and 1 day before expiry. Adding payment via the [billing page](../reference/pricing.md) at any time converts you immediately to the plan you picked at signup; no service interruption.

If the trial expires without a payment method, the tenant is **frozen** — your data is preserved, but no one can sign in until you upgrade. Frozen tenants are kept for 30 days before any data is considered for purge.
