# Pricing

Planscape is priced per firm, not per seat — small teams and growing teams aren't penalised for adding people. All plans include the Revit plugin, the mobile app, the cloud API, ISO 19650 templates, and the audit-grade trail. The differences are seat counts, project counts, storage, and support tier.

| **Studio** | $30 | 1 author + 10 coordinators · 3 projects · 5 GB storage |

**Authors** are users who can sync Revit models from the plugin. **Coordinators** are users who use the web dashboard and the mobile app — viewing models, raising issues, transitioning documents, signing off gates.

A 30-day free trial is available on Studio, Practice, and Network with no payment details required.

## Billing FAQ

**When am I charged?**
On signup (after the trial), then monthly on the same date. Annual billing is available with a 10% discount.

**How do I add a payment method?**
**Settings → Billing → Add payment method**. Mobile money, bank transfer, and card are all supported.

**How do I upgrade or downgrade?**
**Settings → Plan → Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately and are pro-rated against the current cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current cycle, so you don't lose what you've already paid for.

**How do I cancel?**
**Settings → Plan → Cancel subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current cycle; there's no early-termination fee. Your account is frozen (read-only) for 30 days post-cancellation, then purged. We email reminders before each step so nothing is lost in error.

**Can I pause my subscription?**
Yes — Practice and Network plans can be paused for up to 60 days per year. The account stays read-only during the pause and resumes when you click Resume.

**What if I exceed my project or seat count?**
The plugin and mobile app warn the lead user. Existing access continues. New projects or seats trigger an upgrade prompt; you can upgrade in-product without contacting sales.

## Self-host alternative

For teams that prefer to avoid recurring SaaS fees, the Planscape server stack runs as a Docker Compose deployment on your own infrastructure. Self-hosting is included in the Enterprise plan and is also available as a one-time-licence option (contact sales). See the [self-hosting guide](../ops/self-host.md) for technical requirements and walk-through.
