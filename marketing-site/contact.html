Quick answers

How do I report a security vulnerability?
Email security@planscape.app. Please don't open a public GitHub issue. We follow a 90-day coordinated disclosure policy and credit reporters in our security advisories (with your permission).
I'm a journalist — who do I talk to?
Email press@planscape.app. We respond same business day. We have a press kit with logos, screenshots, founder bios, and a one-page company summary on request.
Do you have a status page?
Yes — status.planscape.app. Live API health, scheduled maintenance, post-incident reports.
Can I tour your office?
Yes — drop us a line. We're happy to host AEC firms, students, and partner organisations on weekday mornings. Bring a hard hat if you want to visit the projects we're delivering on.
